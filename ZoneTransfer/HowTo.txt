To use this script, you must give it executable permissions

"sudo chmod 755 zonetransfer.sh"

From there you will run in your Unix terminal:
"./zonetransfer.sh"


This script will gather the NS (Name Servers)records of a domain and request to do a zone transfer. To read more on zone transfers, go to https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DNS_zone_transfer
